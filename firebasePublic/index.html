We're talking to you volunteerhero, Givegabs, VolunteerMatch, and all volunteering websites

ALohadash is a free volunteering platform where vounteers can contribute, share, and view events that they or other communitites have created. Missed an event? Missing crucial hours? Or you just want to make your community better? No problem, ALohadash has your back! Simply create an account or log in, and you'll be able to access your thousands of active events happening your local community, linked to you.

Intuitive UI

We think about user experience first. Our platform is designed to be user friendly. View and search events intuitively with our well-thought intelligent UI design.

Community-Driven

Our platform finds the missing piece of volunteering. We open hundreds of needed events. Contribute by particpating and get paid!
